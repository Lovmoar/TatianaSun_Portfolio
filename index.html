My Projects

Project 1: Perfomance Art

In this self-filmed and edited video, I challenge the idea of being our own worst critic by presenting a more uplifting perspective: "you are enough." Using a Canon camera and Adobe software, I interviewed friends, family, and strangers, asking them to describe me in a few words or a phrase. The video blends these heartfelt responses with my own artistic vision, promoting self-acceptance and validating personal worth through the eyes of others.

Project 3: Gardenia!

Gardenia is a collaborative group project that reimagines classic board games as a charming garden adventure. Our game features a main board shaped like a wooden garden bed with 8x8 holes, and plant-shaped pieces for Tic Tac Toe, Connect 4, and Checkers. Crafted with laser-cut MDF and 3D-printed PLA pieces in black and white, Gardenia offers a fun, travel-sized experience. The board is adorned with garden-themed etchings and storylines that enhance gameplay, making it suitable for all ages 4 and up. Enjoy a refreshing twist on traditional games with our garden-themed narrative!
